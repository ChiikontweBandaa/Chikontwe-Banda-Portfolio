About Chikontwe's Watch Store

Founded in 2021, Chikontwe's Watch Store has been providing quality timepieces to the people of Zambia and beyond. Our passion for watches drives us to curate the finest collection of watches that combine style, functionality, and affordability.

We believe that a watch is more than just a timekeeping device - it's a statement of personal style, a family heirloom, and a companion for life's important moments. That's why we carefully select each watch in our collection, ensuring it meets our high standards of quality and design.

Whether you're looking for a classic timepiece for formal occasions, a durable watch for everyday wear, or a fun design for your child, you'll find it at Chikontwe's Watch Store.
