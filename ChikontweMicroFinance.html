About Us

Our Story

Chikontwe Micro Finance was established in 2023 with a vision to provide financial services to underserved communities. We believe that everyone deserves access to fair financial products that can help them achieve their dreams.

Our mission is to empower individuals and small business owners by providing them with the financial tools and resources they need to succeed. We offer a range of services including microloans, savings accounts, and financial education.

With a focus on personalized service and community development, we're proud to be a trusted partner in our clients' financial journeys.
